Hmmm... PeeWeeLinux doesn't use shadow or PAM. If you get the login prompt
you would have login and mingetty. Make sure you have /var/run/utmp and
/var/log/wtmp. If you don't have those you're missing an important
package...

I am cc'ing this to the PeeWeeLinux list just in case I am missing
something here.
