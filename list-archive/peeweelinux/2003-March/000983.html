Hi,

Sorry for that last email. After many many google searches I finally stumbled onto something interesting. My problem was that my mount command woudl hang for several minutes.

With a 2.4 kernel (and possibly other kernels...) compiled with NFS support for client, all you need is to configure fstab to NOT use the lockd program. So, in your fstab options, pass mount the "nolock" option and now you can mount and unmount NFS shares with peewee...
