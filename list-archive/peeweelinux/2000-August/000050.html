Hi,

Sure, you just need to connect the harddrive to the development system.
Then you can use pwlconfig to build the system and load onto the
harddrive. It's a bit awkward if your target machine is not the same. But
for development purposes it works great, if the HD to run PWL is actually
/dev/hda and your development system dualboots from another drive.
