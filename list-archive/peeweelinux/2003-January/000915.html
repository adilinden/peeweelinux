You are looking for a RTOS. PeeWeeLinux is simply a standard linux embedded distribution (or target builder, however you look at it). 

I have used PeeWeeLinux successfully with RTAI and RTLinux compiled kernels that allow for Deterministic hard realtime. Check out  www.rtai.org  for more info. After learning RTAI you can build an embedded image with Peewee.
