Look in ${PWL}/packages/System_Base/, the "util-linux" package. 
Basically, untar util-linux.tar, edit the etc/passwd and associated 
files, and retar them back up.

Make sure to "Extract Filesystem for project" again to repopulate the 
'built' filesystem before transferring to flashdisk...
