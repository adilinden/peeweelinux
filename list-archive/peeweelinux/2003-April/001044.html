what I learned from the manufacturers:

A compact flash card is good for about 500,000 writes per sector. Industrial
strength cards suggest about 2,000,000 writes per sector.

So, it depends on your usage.
I use it to store a ram disk image, and configuration files to limit the
number of writes to the card.
