Good news for linux embedded developers,
As a part of this growing community we felt that we need to support the 
developers. We redesigned a product for developers, based on the Geode GX1 
processor. Details:

NS GEODE GX1 32 BIT CPU MMX,200MHz(can overclock upto 266MHz)/4x10-100 LAN.
It supports the following: RS232/2.5"IDE/Disk on chip/IRport/built in 
flash/PCI expansion slot/mouse/keyboard/parallel port/line in-out,reset 
button.

Mail me for more details. Dont forget to mention number of units you need.
